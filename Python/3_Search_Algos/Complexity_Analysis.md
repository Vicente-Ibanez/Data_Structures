Complexity Analysis
- Method of determining the efficiency of algorithms
- allows you to rate them independently of platform-dependent timings or impractical instruction counts

Orders of Complexity 
Best (slowest growth)
1. Constant (no change) O(n)
2. Logarithmic Olog(n)
3. Linear O(1)
4. Linear-Logarithmic O(nlog(n))
4. Quadratic O(n^2)
5. Exponential O(2^c) etc
6. 
Worst (fastest growth)

We only care about the dominat term
    - Ignore smaller terms 

Asymptotic Analysis
    - Look at growth as n approaches infinity

Constant of Proportionality
    - You can ignore the coefficients and boil growth down to just the term 
    - ex: O(5n) can be just O(n)
